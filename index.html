About

Puerto Rican flutist, educator, and fullstack developer. Graduated from the Conservatory of Music in Puerto Rico in 2017 and holds a degree in Flute Performance. And, learned to code in Kansas University Fullstack Developer Bootcamp in 2025.

Ivelis has performed as a soloist in the Anthony “Junior” Soto Hall in the Conservatorio de Música and Gallery Inn Hotel in Old San Juan with a high-level classical flute repertoire. She also participated in the Aruba Symphony Orchestra where she studied with Maria Gabriela Rodriguez (2016) and Alea21 (2016-2019), and in the Puerto Rico Summer Symphony Orchestra (2017). She performed as a soloist again at the First Presbyterian Church in Junction City (2022) and Kirmser Hall in Kansas State University, Manhattan (2023) . Ivelis had her first debut as a soloist with the Heart of America Band (2024).

She has participated in masterclasses with renowned flutists such as Thomas Beyer, Dr. Sarah Frisof, Marcos Granado, Alhelí Pimienta, Elena Yakovleva, James Strauss, María Gabriela Rodriguez. Richard Sherman, and Wendy Rolfe.
